### 🛒 ABS Retail — End-to-End Data Analytics 
> *The flagship project. Production-grade, full lifecycle analytics.*

[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Full%20Project-0D1B2A?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/abs-retail-analytics)
[![Power BI](https://img.shields.io/badge/Power%20BI%20Dashboard-F2C811?style=for-the-badge&logo=powerbi&logoColor=black)](https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/abs-retail-analytics/tree/main/dashboard)

**Business Problem:** ABS Retail operates across 23 countries with a return rate of 8.62% (costing ~$145K/year), a repeat customer rate of only 34.28% (vs 50% target), and a portfolio misaligned toward low-margin products.

**What I Built:**
- 🐍 Python EDA and predictive forecasting script (pandas, matplotlib, numpy)
- 📊 6-page interactive Power BI dashboard with 20+ visuals, DAX measures, AI Key Influencers, and a 2025 revenue forecast with confidence intervals
- 📋 Excel analysis workbook — 8 formatted sheets with KPI tables, trend charts and pivot data
- 🎯 6 strategic recommendations with quantified ROI estimates
- 📑 16-slide professional stakeholder presentation

**Key Findings:**
| Metric | Finding | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Return Rate | 8.62% vs 5% target | ~$145K/year cost |
| Repeat Rate | 34.28% vs 50% target | ~$400K/year gap |
| Category Margin | Beauty & Health earns 57.5% vs Electronics 21.9% | Portfolio misaligned |
| Growth | 2024 revenue +15.3% YoY | Recovery confirmed |
| Forecast 2025 | $705K–$740K (CAGR + Optimistic) | 0.4%–5.2% growth |

**Tech Stack:**
`Python` `pandas` `matplotlib` `numpy` `Power BI` `DAX` `Excel` `python-pptx`

## 🧠 My Analytical Approach

Every project I deliver follows the same rigorous framework:

```
1. UNDERSTAND THE BUSINESS        → What decision does this analysis support?
         ↓
2. AUDIT THE DATA                 → Quality checks, nulls, types, distributions
         ↓
3. DECLARE LIMITATIONS            → What CAN'T the data tell us? (Senior-level transparency)
         ↓
4. ENGINEER FEATURES              → Create calculated fields that answer the business questions
         ↓
5. EXPLORE & ANALYZE              → Descriptive → Diagnostic → Predictive
         ↓
6. VISUALIZE                      → Charts that communicate, not just display
         ↓
7. QUANTIFY IMPACT                → Every insight has a dollar or percentage attached
         ↓
8. RECOMMEND & PRIORITIZE         → URGENT / HIGH / MEDIUM / PLAN with ROI estimates
```

> *"A great analyst does not just tell you what happened. They tell you why it happened, what will happen next, and what you should do about it."*
