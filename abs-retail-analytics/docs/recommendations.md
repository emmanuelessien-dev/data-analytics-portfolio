## REC 01 — Reduce Return Rate Below 5% 🔴 URGENT

**Current State:** 8.62% return rate (431 orders) vs 5.0% benchmark
**Estimated Annual Cost:** ~$145,000 in lost/reversed revenue

### Evidence
- **Changed Mind (200 cases, 46%):** The leading return reason is preventable — customers are not getting enough information pre-purchase to make confident decisions
- **Size Issues (149 cases, 35%):** Concentrated in Clothing & Apparel — a structural UX gap
- **Defective Items (125 cases, 29%):** Points to supplier quality control failures, particularly in Electronics

### Recommended Actions
1. **Add 360° product photography + short video demos** to all Electronics and Clothing listings — target: reduce "Changed Mind" by 40%
2. **Implement AI-powered size guide** with AR try-on for Clothing & Apparel — modelled on ASOS size advisor
3. **Introduce mandatory pre-ship QC inspection** for all Electronics orders above $200
4. **Conduct supplier audit** for top 5 Electronics vendors — defective rate should be < 1%
5. **Revamp product description template** — add: dimensions, weight, compatibility, customer photos

### Expected Impact
- Return rate: 8.62% → 5.5% within 12 months → 5.0% within 24 months
- Revenue recovered: ~$52,000–$72,000 annually

## REC 02 — Launch a Loyalty Program to Close the Retention Gap 🔴 CRITICAL

**Current State:** 34.28% repeat rate vs 50%+ target
**Revenue at Stake:** Est. $400,000+ per year in uncaptured lifetime value

### Evidence
- 65.72% of customers never returned — the majority of the customer base is single-transaction
- Professional segment (highest AOV at $558.49) has relatively better repeat behaviour — but still below target
- Mobile App users show higher repeat rates than Website users — channel affects retention

### Recommended Actions
1. **Launch ABS Loyalty Points Program** — tiered: Bronze (1× points), Silver (1.5×), Gold (2×) based on annual spend
2. **Post-purchase email automation** — deploy a 3-email sequence at Day 7 (review request), Day 30 (personalised reorder suggestion), Day 60 (loyalty reward reminder)
3. **Target Professional segment first** — highest AOV and most likely to respond to B2B loyalty incentives
4. **Mobile App exclusive rewards** — push notifications + app-only flash sales to drive repeat Mobile App orders
5. **Personalised recommendations engine** — "Customers like you also bought..." on post-delivery confirmation emails

### Expected Impact
- Repeat rate: 34.28% → 42% within 12 months → 50% within 24 months
- Revenue upside: $200,000–$400,000 annually at current AOV levels

## REC 03 — Rebalance Category Investment Toward High-Margin Lines 🟡 HIGH

**Current State:** Electronics = 56.4% revenue share but only 21.9% gross margin

### Evidence

| Category | Revenue Share | Gross Margin | Absolute Profit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Electronics | 56.4% | 21.9% | $325,793 |
| Home & Living | 21.6% | 35.2% | $200,383 |
| Beauty & Health | 11.4% | **57.5%** | $172,973 |
| Clothing & Apparel | 7.7% | 50.7% | $103,170 |
| Books & Media | 2.9% | 45.2% | $34,629 |

Beauty & Health earns 2.6× the margin rate of Electronics.

### Recommended Actions
1. **Shift 15% of Electronics ad spend to Beauty & Health** — model: reduce Electronics paid media budget, increase Beauty influencer/social campaigns
2. **Bundle strategy** — pair Beauty & Health products with Electronics purchases ("Complete your setup" cross-sells)
3. **Expand Beauty & Health SKU range** — currently underrepresented vs industry average product depth
4. **Introduce Books & Media bundling or subscription model** — low AOV category needs volume or subscription economics to justify shelf space

### Expected Impact
- Portfolio gross margin: 31.74% → 35–38% within 24 months
- Absolute annual profit increase: $80,000–$120,000

## REC 04 — Accelerate Mobile App Channel Investment 🟡 HIGH

**Current State:** Mobile App ($803,043) is rapidly closing gap on Website ($949,254)

### Evidence
- Mobile App grew faster YoY than any other channel in 2023–2024
- Mobile App users show higher repeat purchase rates
- Social Media ($372K) and Marketplace ($330K) are underperforming relative to traffic potential

### Recommended Actions
1. **App-exclusive flash sales** — 24-hour deals only accessible in the app; drives downloads and engagement
2. **Push notification campaigns** — personalised, behaviour-triggered (abandon cart, reorder reminder, loyalty points alert)
3. **Social Media → App conversion funnel** — run paid Social ads that drive directly to app download, not website
4. **App onboarding flow improvement** — reduce checkout friction; target < 3 taps from browse to purchase

### Expected Impact
- Mobile App channel share: 30.5% → 40% within 24 months
- Social Media conversion: +20% of Social traffic redirected to app (higher lifetime value customers)

## REC 05 — Invest in Oceania & Expand Asia Coverage 🟢 MEDIUM

**Current State:** Oceania has 445 orders (8.9% of total) but the highest AOV at $549 — a premium signal

### Evidence
- Oceania generates $244K revenue from only 445 orders — most efficient revenue-per-order continent
- Asia leads in volume (1,098 orders) but ABS only covers 6 Asian countries out of 49 in the region
- Africa is the second-largest revenue continent — largely Nigeria-driven; 22 other African markets are untapped

### Recommended Actions
1. **Launch targeted digital campaigns in Australia and New Zealand** — English-language markets, high purchasing power, premium segment fit
2. **Expand Asia from 6 → 15 countries** — priority: South Korea, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines
3. **Africa expansion** — beyond Nigeria: Kenya, Ghana, Egypt, South Africa (already present), Ethiopia
4. **Localised payment methods** — add M-Pesa for East Africa, GCash for Philippines, PromptPay for Thailand

### Expected Impact
- Oceania revenue: $244K → $380K with targeted investment (+56%)
- Asia revenue: $609K → $820K with country expansion (+35%)

## REC 06 — Set 2025 Revenue Target at $705K–$740K 🔵 PLAN

**Current State:** 2024 revenue $702,533 — strongest in 4 years

### Forecast Scenarios

| Scenario | 2025 Target | Key Assumption |
|---|---|---|
| Conservative | $607,000 | Market headwinds, no new initiatives |
| Baseline (Linear) | $660,000 | Current trajectory maintained |
| CAGR Model | $705,000 | 0.4% CAGR applied |
| Optimistic | $739,000 | Loyalty + return reduction implemented |

### Recommended Actions
1. **Set official 2025 target at $720,000** — stretch but achievable with Recs 01–03 implemented
2. **February campaign focus** — Feb is the weakest month every year; invest in "Valentine's + Clearance" promotion to lift the trough
3. **July–August peak maximisation** — pre-load inventory and launch promotions 3 weeks before the seasonal peak
4. **Quarterly performance reviews** — use Power BI dashboard to track against monthly projections (available in Forecast page)
